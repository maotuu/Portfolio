WORK ABOUT

Apsara

A mock fund trading platform visualzing and decoding fund data and trends

Duration

Feb.-Jul. 2016 @ State Street
Check the iPad App out on App Store

Methods and Tools

Business Analysis, Storyboarding, Wireframing, Sketch, Photoshop, Axure PR, HTML/CSS

Brief

Apsara is an online platform for individual investors who would like to simulate and analyze their investment funds. It also helps users to learn about fund investment methods in order to improve upon and sharpen their investment skills.

My Role

As the UX design intern, except from making quick and subtle improvements to the existing product, I was mainly involved the restructure and redesign of Apsara.

My work included,
1. Worked with BA and PM on strategic design decisions and determinded the requirements.
2.To better help users learn about the investment methods and sharpen their investment skills, designed visualizations decoding huge amount of fund data.
3. Redesigned the main task flows and came up with wireframs, and high-fidelity interfaces.
4. Using Axure PR, delivered an interactive prototype and design documents.
